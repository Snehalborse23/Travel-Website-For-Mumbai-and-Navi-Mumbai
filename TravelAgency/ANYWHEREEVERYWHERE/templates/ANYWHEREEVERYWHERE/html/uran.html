URAN

Uran is a coastal town and part of Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra state.The primary languages spoken are Agri and Koli which is a dialect of Marathi-Konkani.Uran is surrounded by sea on three sides.Places to invist in Uran :

-> Pirwad Beach

The scenic Pirwadi Beach is supposed to be one of the most visited beaches in Uran, which also lies in close proximity to the Mankeshwar Beach renowned for a Lord Shiva Temple.Furthermore, this beach also provides for magnificent views of the “setting sun”. In addition, there are number of temples that are situated around this serene beach.It has a plaza hotel nearby where tourist can stay or can have lunch/dinner for those coming for go-to destination.

-> Shamiyana Restaurant(Veg/Non-Veg)

This is well-known establishment as servicing customers both local and from other parts of Navi Mumbai. It is known to provide top service in North Indian, Indian Restaurants. Ramratan Shamiyana has a wide range of products and services to cater to varied requirements of their customers. The staff are courteous and prompt at providing any assistance. This establishment is functional from 11:00-23:00

-> Vaishnavi Restaurant(Pure Veg)

This is a Pure Veg Restaurant serves only vegetarian food. They offer a variety of delicious food from soups to main course to desserts. If you are on the hunt for a pure veg restaurant located in Uran, Navi Mumbai, this is the best to visit. It is a popular choice for people who indulge in vegetarian food.

-> Dronagiri Fort

The fort is situated on the top of the hill which is close to Uran town in Raigad district.The fort is accessible in all seasons. The trek to the fort is simple and safe.The History of this fort is that the fort was under the rule of Yadav of Devgiri.The entire path passes through dense forest of Gliricidia sepium or Mexican Lilac trees planted by Forest department.There is a Police Chowki near the Church on the fort. Night halt on the fort is restricted.

-> Bunkerzz Cafe

Its a Cafe where everyone can come and enjoy.Calm Place to Sit and have some tasty Snacks.Proper place for the youngsters who are looking for a good place to chill with their friends with free wifi service.These have a warm ambience and seating arrangements for you to enjoy.

-> Vimla Talao

It is a small Garden located in the town named Uran.Good place to have a walk either in the morning else evening you can do yoga, meditate, walk, play according to your mood.There are small rides for the kids to play and have small food stalls around the garden.Also Vimla talao has got rich heritage, ancient culture in the surrounding area with Chattrapati Shivaji Maharaj Statue.
